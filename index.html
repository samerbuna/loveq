How to play the game.

This is meant to be played in real life / face-to-face with 2 people, though you could experiment with more. Your partner could be a romantic interest, adventurous friend, current lover, or anyone you'd be curious and comfortable developing deeper intimacy with. Each of the following cards has a conversational prompt - simply follow each one thoughtfully and authentically.

Give yourself 90 minutes or so -- perfect for a first date or date night. The app works on desktop as well as mobile. Let's play!
